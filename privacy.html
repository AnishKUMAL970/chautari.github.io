Privacy Policy for Chautari

Effective Date: July 28, 2025

Chautari is a social media management tool designed to help users manage, schedule, and analyze their social media presence across multiple platforms including Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, and YouTube.

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic profile information (name, email) from connected accounts
  • Content posted or scheduled through our platform
  • Analytics data for account growth and engagement

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To schedule and post content to your connected accounts
  • To provide insights and analytics
  • To improve our services and user experience

3. Third-Party Access

We use third-party APIs to access your account data with your permission. These include:

  • Facebook Graph API
  • Instagram Basic Display API
  • LinkedIn API
  • TikTok for Developers API
  • YouTube Data API

We do not sell or share your data with unauthorized third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All access tokens and sensitive data are stored securely and encrypted.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to view, edit, and delete your data at any time. See the User Data Deletion page for more information.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at support@chautariapp.com.
